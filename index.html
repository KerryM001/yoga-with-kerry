About Yoga with Kerry

Before I found yoga, I was constantly tired and stressed from the pressures of modern life. I completed my Vinyasa training in 2017. I then travelled to India and Bali to further my understanding of the practice. Certified with Yoga Ireland, I've has been teaching yoga all over the world for the past 5 years. The daily practice of yoga can add so much to your life. It can teach you how to really slow down, and appreciate the present moment. Regarding of your level of knowledge, all are welcome at my classes.
