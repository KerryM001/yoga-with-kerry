About Yoga with Kerry

Before I found yoga, I was constantly tired and stressed from the pressures of modern life. I completed my Vinyasa training in 2017. I then travelled to India and Bali to further my understanding of the practice. Certified with Yoga Ireland, I've has been teaching yoga all over the world for the past 5 years.
